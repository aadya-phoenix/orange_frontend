Before submitting your request to become part of OBS SME community, please take the time to read our covenant and acknowledging it:

PDL SME Community covenant

SME Community Mission

The SME Community is one of the enablers for OBS to become a Learning organization by enabling a co-development environment through sharing knowledge and hands-on experience on the latest industry technologies and practices.

As a SME Community Member

• Agree to transform your own knowledge into an OBS knowledge by providing training materials [Powerpoint, participant guide and recorded session audio or video] to be owned by OBS/PD&L.

• Dedicate 100% of the community allocated time to the agreed learning activity.

• Prepare and arrange for the training material for use in classroom, face to face or virtual including pre and post testing and evaluation forms.

• Follow all PD&L processes, procedures including the use of the company learning management system (Orange Learning) and PD&L templates.

• Restrict the use of the knowledge acquired within Orange PDL SME community to the internal usage within Orange. External use would be limited to publicly available content.

• Not to participate in delivery of any external learning activity externally involving Orange Employees.

As a SME Community Leader

• Animate and lead the operation of your domain SME Learning Community.

• Appoint trainers for the different missions and needs.

• Manage membership of current and new members for the community [Refreshment of members, additions of new members and removal of members]

• Review with PD&L the evaluations and feedbacks from learning activities by SMEs and plan improvements accordingly.

• Ensuring trainer’s within your community follow PD&L process for Training delivery and post training activity.
